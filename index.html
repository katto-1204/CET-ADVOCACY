Overview

EmpowerED is a campaign focused on bridging the gap between curiosity and technological literacy. Through interactive booths, hands-on challenges, and collaborative spaces, we guide students from curiosity to practical skills that enable innovation and teamwork.

Objectives

  1. Introduce students to CETSO and its child organizations, showcasing their academic programs and opportunities.
  2. Build technology literacy through guided, hands-on activities that establish a strong foundation in tech concepts.
  3. Engage students in mini challenges and collaborative tasks that strengthen teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
  4. Encourage active participation and promote membership in CETSO organizations to support personal and academic growth.
  5. Highlight how inter-organization collaboration drives personal, academic, and professional development.
